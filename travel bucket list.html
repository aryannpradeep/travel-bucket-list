India Travel Bucket List

Explore the Wonders of India

Jaipur, Rajasthan

The Pink City known for its royal palaces and forts.

Goa

Famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders.

Kerala

Known for lush greenery, backwaters, and houseboats.

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Scenic hill station with snowy peaks and adventure sports.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Spiritual capital of India, famous for the Ganga Aarti.

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Home to Bollywood, the Gateway of India, and vibrant life.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Known for its tea gardens and breathtaking mountain views.

Nainital, Uttarakhand

A picturesque town famous for its lake and scenic views.
